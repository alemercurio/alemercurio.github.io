Alessandro Mercurio

Hello there! I'm Alessandro, and this is my personal webpage. Welcome!

I am a software engineer and a roboticist.

I graduated from a Bachelor of Science in Engineering of Computing Systems at Politecnico di Milano, in Italy.

I am about to obtain a Master's degree in in Robotics, Systems and Control from ETH Zurich in Switzerland. During my studies I have been a graduate student researcher at the Robotic Systems Lab under Prof. Dr. Marco Hutter and I wrote my Master Thesis at the Computer Vision and Geometry Group under Prof. Dr. Pollefeys. Furthermore, I built and programmed a driverless car with the incredible AMZ Racing team.

Last but definetly not least, I am currently a researcher at Sony AI, and I have worked at both the Tokyo and Zurich offices.
